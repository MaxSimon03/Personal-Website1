Hi there! My name is Maxwell Simon and I am an aspiring UI/UX & Software developer. I started my journey in computing when I was 11 years old, my first ever project was building my own computer with my dad. It was then that I realized my love for technology.

After completing a year in the Computer Science program at Brock University, I decided to switch to the Multi Media Design & Development program at Humber College, where I am currently pursuing my dreams of becoming a UI/UX developer.

I have been proficient in using Photoshop for the past 10 years now and it has been an integral part of my design process. My coding journey began 3 years ago when I learned my first programming language, Java. Since then, I have expanded my skills to include other languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, React & Tailwind,. I am constantly learning and improving my skills as a developer and designer, and I am excited to see where this journey takes me.
